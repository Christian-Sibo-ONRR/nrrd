# Download Data & Documentation
You can download data for production volumes, revenue, and disbursements here. 
All data is from the [Office of Natural Resources Revenue](https://www.onrr.gov/).

## Production
---
### Production by month
This dataset includes commodity volumes of natural resources extracted from federal lands and waters and Native American lands by month. 
We offer data from January 2008 through the most recently available month.
<DownloadDataLink to="/downloads/federal-production-by-month/">Downloads and documentation</DownloadDataLink>

### Production by year
This dataset includes commodity volumes of natural resources extracted from Native American lands and federal lands and waters. 
Production volumes are available by state, county, and offshore area for federal production data. Location data is <GlossaryTerm>withheld</GlossaryTerm>
for Native American data. We offer federal production data for calendar and fiscal years. We offer Native American production for fiscal year only.
<DownloadDataLink to="/downloads/federal-production/">Downloads and documentation</DownloadDataLink>

## Revenue
---
### Revenue by month
This dataset is for revenue by month for January 2008 through the most recently completed month of the current year. The data includes federal 
offshore and onshore revenue, along with revenue from Native American lands. The data is further broken down by revenue type (i.e., bonuses, rents, and royalties) 
and commodity.
<DownloadDataLink to="/downloads/federal-revenue-by-month/">Downloads and documentation</DownloadDataLink>

### Revenue by year
This dataset includes natural resource revenue for Native American lands and U.S. federal lands and offshore areas by calendar and fiscal years. 
It does not include privately owned lands or state-owned lands.
<DownloadDataLink to="/downloads/federal-revenue-by-location/">Downloads and documentation</DownloadDataLink>

### Federal revenue by company
This dataset provides natural resource revenue by company. It includes revenues by production phase and commodity for companies extracting natural resources on
 federal lands and waters. It does not include company revenue from Native American lands or privately owned lands.
<DownloadDataLink to="/downloads/federal-revenue-by-company/">Downloads and documentation</DownloadDataLink>

## Disbursements
---
### Disbursements by month
The amount of money earned from extraction of natural resources on federal lands that is disbursed to various legislated funds every month.
<DownloadDataLink to="/downloads/disbursements-by-month/">Downloads and documentation</DownloadDataLink>

### Disbursements by year
The amount of money earned from extraction of natural resources on federal lands that is disbursed to various legislated funds by fiscal year.
<DownloadDataLink to="/downloads/disbursements/">Downloads and documentation</DownloadDataLink>
