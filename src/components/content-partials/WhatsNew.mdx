## What's new

In our latest release on August 21, 2020, we made the following changes:

- Added [calendar year 2019 revenue by company data file](https://beta-nrrd.app.cloud.gov/downloads/federal-revenue-by-company)
- Updated [monthly production data through April 2020](https://beta-nrrd.app.cloud.gov/downloads/production-by-month)
- Updated [monthly revenue data through July 2020](https://beta-nrrd.app.cloud.gov/downloads/revenue-by-month)
- Updated [monthly disbursements data through June 2020](https://beta-nrrd.app.cloud.gov/downloads/disbursements-by-month)
- Addressed bugs and mobile experience on explore data and updated filters on query data
- Updated outside links in [how revenue works](https://beta-nrrd.app.cloud.gov/how-revenue-works)

Review our [full release details](https://github.com/ONRR/nrrd/releases).
